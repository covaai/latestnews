Recent updates 

Refreshed brokers' assistant - now live. 

We've recently updated our brokers' assistant to help you work smarter and close deals faster. Key improvements include:

Full chat history and conversation search, so you never lose important client requirements or past discussions

Enhanced AI model that better understands your needs, providing more accurate and relevant responses

 

Create podcasts within Cova Compare.  

When you log in to our policy comparison tool Cova Compare, you can now create podcasts from the uploaded materials as well as analyse policy documents. If you haven't used this before, there's still time to use your CBN activation code when signing up which is available in the network portal.

To get started click here.

 

An early preview of the all new Cova iOS and Android app is coming soon. 

We're in the early the days of building the Cova app, and will update with a preview in the coming weeks. Some designs include recording in person meeting notes, accessing the assistants from your phone, turning clients websites into podcasts and much more.
